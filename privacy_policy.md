# Privacy Policy — Portfolio Privacy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

Portfolio Privacy is a personal investment tracking app. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Portfolio Privacy does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There are no user accounts, no registration, and no analytics or tracking of any kind. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or tracking libraries.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All investment data you enter (assets, trades, prices) is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Network Requests

The app makes outbound network requests solely to retrieve financial market data and news on your behalf:

- **Yahoo Finance** — current prices and historical price data for assets in your portfolio
- **CoinDesk** — cryptocurrency news headlines
- **Investing.com** — general financial news headlines

These requests are read-only. No personal data, portfolio data, or device identifiers are included in these requests. The app fetches publicly available data using standard RSS feeds and public APIs.

## Backup

If you use the backup feature, a JSON file containing your portfolio data is saved to your device or shared via your device's standard share functionality. This file is handled entirely by you and your device's operating system. We have no access to it.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new date at the top.
